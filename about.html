WHO ARE WE?

We are an ICEF certified education/visa advisory agency in Nigeria. Our commitment is to ensuring that expert advice is given on any aspect of international education. We secure admission and assist in visa processing for prospective students into high schools, colleges and universities overseas.

Making an application is not an easy process, and you might want some advice and support during the visa and application processes to make sure you complete the application correctly.
Why not let EEAS help you with that? We will guide you step by step all the way, give us a call today and you will forever be glad you did. We make the complex processes simple.
